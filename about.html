About Ramadhan World

Ramadhan World is a digital initiative created with love and devotion to make Islamic resources more accessible during the blessed month of Ramadhan and beyond.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help Muslims around the world stay spiritually connected through Quran, Azan reminders, Islamic knowledge, and community-driven content. We aim to provide accurate, respectful, and useful Islamic material in a simple and beautiful digital format.

What We Offer

  • Prayer times based on your location
  • Azan alerts and reminders
  • Quran and Surah access
  • Islamic calendar and event highlights
  • Clean, ad-supported design to keep it free for all

Who We Are

We are a small team of developers and Islamic content creators passionate about spreading knowledge and building tools that help the Ummah in their daily ibadah and routine.

Whether you're starting your spiritual journey or deepening your existing practice, Ramadhan World is here to assist you with modern tools rooted in timeless values.
